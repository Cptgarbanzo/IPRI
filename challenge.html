The Challenge Dice System

Front Desk   Fiction   Non-Fiction   Periodicals   Maps   Multimedia   System  

The Challenge dice system uses six sided dice, there are two types of challenges in the system. The first, the static challenge is a number of required success to complete the challenge, a success on a die is a 4 5 or 6 on a roll. Example, static 5 challenge requires 5 successes to complete the challenge. The Second, The dice off, each participant rolls their dice, and compares, from highest to lowest their numbers with ties going to the defender of the challenge. The person who has the most successes over the other wins. Depending on the type of opposed challenge, it can have detrimental effects on the loser of the challenge.
